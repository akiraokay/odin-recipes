Tamagoyaki

A traditional Japanese dish that is quick to prepare.

  • 5 eggs
  • 5 tablespoons sugar
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  1. Beat all ingredients together in a bowl
  2. Using a ladel, pour a small amount of the mixture into the square pan, enough for a layer a few millimetres thick to form.
  3. Before the mixture cooks through, slide it to the far end of the pan, then place another ladel of mixture in the newly created space.
  4. Before the new layer cooks through, roll the old block of egg over itself and into the new layer.
  5. Slide the new, bigger block to the far edge of the pan, and repeat until you have used all the egg.
